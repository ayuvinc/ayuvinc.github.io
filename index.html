The Problem

Healthcare treats visits.
Not patients.

Every time a patient changes doctor, clinic, or city — their medical history stays behind. Every new doctor starts from zero. India's healthcare system has no memory.

600K+
Independent clinics in India, each owning their own data silo — disconnected from each other and from the patient.
₹22B+
Annual prescription and pharma flow happening on paper, with no audit trail, no continuity, no intelligence.
Zero
Longitudinal patient records across India's independent clinic network. Every new appointment starts from scratch.

"At 17, my kidneys failed. My father gave me his. But my records didn't follow me — they came in hundreds of loose papers, scattered across clinics, carried in bags, stuffed into folders, recalled from memory at every new appointment. For 17 years, I was my own medical record system."

— Aditya Kaushal, Founder & CEO

Launch Products

Live Beta
Dhara धारा
"flow" — AI Clinical OS for India's independent doctors

The patient owns their record — not the clinic. Every consultation, prescription, and diagnosis follows the patient for life, across every doctor and every city. Built ABDM-native from day one — consent tokens issued per patient, not per institution.

ABDM-native · ABHA consent tokens · ₹199/month · Competitors cannot retrofit this — it is the moat.
Live Beta
Vaani वाणी by Dhara
"voice" — Real-time AI clinical scribe

Transcribes the live consultation, generates a structured clinical summary, and waits for the doctor's sign-off before saving anything. The Doctor Approval Gate is non-negotiable — every AI output is a suggestion, nothing more.

Powered by Sarvam AI · 73.3% gross margin · Avg consult: 2.1 min · Sold standalone and with Dhara
Demo · Pilot Stage
Setu सेतु
"bridge" — Transplant coordination platform

Replaces paper forms and WhatsApp chains with structured, auditable workflows for NOTTO-registered hospitals — waitlist management, donor workup, cold ischemia timers, and compliance reporting.

THOTA-aligned · No automated allocation · 10-year audit trail · Target: 250+ NOTTO centres

"hand" — Hospital navigation for patients and families

When someone is admitted, their family doesn't know the system. Haath connects families with vetted hospital social-service agents in real time. Unlocks after Phase 1 reaches EBITDA positive.
