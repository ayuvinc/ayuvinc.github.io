The Problem

Healthcare treats visits.
Not patients.

Every time a patient changes doctor, clinic, or city — their medical history stays behind. Every new doctor starts from zero. India's healthcare system has no memory.

1.7%
EMR adoption across India's independent clinics. The rest still run on paper.
600K
Independent clinics, each owning their own data silo — disconnected from each other and from the patient.
₹22B+
Annual prescription and pharma flow happening on paper, with no audit trail, no continuity, no intelligence.

What We're Building

Three products.
One architecture.

Patient-owned from day one. Built on ABDM rails. Designed so no competitor can retrofit the moat.

Live Beta 🏥
Dhara
धारा — "flow"

AI Clinical OS for India's independent doctors. The patient owns their record — not the clinic. Every consultation, prescription, and diagnosis follows the patient for life, across every doctor and every city.

✦ Featured: Vaani (वाणी)

Real-time AI clinical scribe. Vaani transcribes the live consultation, generates a structured summary, and waits for the doctor's sign-off before saving anything. Average consult time: 2.1 minutes. The Doctor Approval Gate is non-negotiable — every AI output is a suggestion, nothing more.

ABDM-native · ABHA consent tokens per patient · ₹199/month entry price · 73.3% gross margin on Vaani sessions
Demo · Pilot Stage 🔗
Setu
सेतु — "bridge"

AI-assisted transplant coordination platform for NOTTO-registered hospitals. Replaces paper forms and WhatsApp chains with structured, auditable workflows — waitlist management, donor workup, cold ischemia timers, and NOTTO compliance reporting.

THOTA-aligned · Administrative support only — no automated allocation · 10-year immutable audit trail · Target: 250+ NOTTO centres
Phase 2 🤝
Haath
हाथ — "hand"

Hospital navigation for patients and families. When someone is admitted, their family doesn't know the system — which ward, which form, which agent to trust. Haath connects families with vetted hospital social-service agents in real time.

Unlocks after Phase 1 reaches EBITDA positive · In-patients + caregivers · Vetted agent network

Our Story

Three founders.
Three failures of the same system.

We didn't find this problem in a market report. We lived it.

Aditya Kaushal
Founder & CEO

"Two kidney transplants. Five surgeries across three countries. In 2022, I was alone in a Tunisian ICU — my records were in India, on paper, in a bag at home. In 2024, my mother gave me her kidney after an 11-month wait. For 15 years, I was my own medical record system. I built this because I lived every failure it fixes."

Raghu Raja Sharma
Co-Founder & CTO

"On the day my son was born, my mother had a stroke. I had spent 14 years building enterprise systems at JP Morgan, Cigna, and HSBC — systems that moved billions of dollars across 7 countries. I had never felt more helpless than navigating a hospital with no map, no record, no system."

Dr. Komal Sharma
Co-Founder & Chief Clinical Officer

"Every day in clinic, my patients bring years of history in plastic bags — photographs of old prescriptions, half-remembered diagnoses. They have seen seven doctors and none of them knew what the others said. The Doctor Approval Gate in Dhara is my clinical principle: AI is a tool, not a decision-maker."

"We are not building an EMR. We are building the OS for healthcare in India."
